A dawn run along the ridge line, flown low enough to catch the frost still on the grass. The FPV pass was flown twice: once for the chase line itself, once purely for the sound of the motors against the wind, cut in underneath the wider shots from the A7SIII further down the slope.

Weather window was under twenty minutes before the light went flat. Everything here is a first take.
