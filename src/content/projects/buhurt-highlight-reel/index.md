Buhurt is a modern full-contact combat sport — competitors in historically accurate medieval armor, fighting with blunted steel weapons. The term comes from the Old French *béhourd*, roughly "to wallop" or "to batter." It's an accurate description.

Covered on the ground with the Lumix and both GoPros, and from above with the cinewhoop and Insta360 for the moments that needed room to breathe. A brutal sport to compete in, a thrilling one to shoot.
